About Me

My name is Mike Garcia and I am an upcoming Web Developer! I just finished Week 1 of SMU Coding Bootcamp. Although the pacing is fast, I have been able to keep up with the pace! At least for now...

Prior to Web Development, I was a percussion director for seven years. I had the opportunity to work with students from 6th-12th grade and teach percussion all day everyday! I loved it! While I have learned so much from them, and from the world-class staff over the years, I was ready to learn some new skills focusing on technology!

I am excited for what's in store as a Web Developer! Even though I've only been immersed in this material for a week, I feel like I have gained so much knowledge! I can't wait to see what else there is to learn in the big world of Web Development!
